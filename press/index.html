Boilerplate (EN)

MediGlobe is a mobile medication reference for travellers. Type the name or active ingredient of a familiar drug and instantly see its local equivalents in 13 countries — brand names, dosages and warnings — fully offline and in 10 languages. All reference and emergency content is free; AI-powered tools (package scanner, symptom checker, interaction checker, dose calculator) are available by subscription. Personal medical data never leaves the device. Launching soon on Google Play, with iOS to follow.
