Information in the app (including AI answers) may contain inaccuracies and must not be your sole basis for decisions about health, intake, dosage, or combining medications.

In an emergency, call your local emergency number immediately. Always consult a qualified professional. You use the app at your own risk — see the Terms of Use for details.

1. Who We Are

MediGlobe is an independent mobile application developed by the MediGlobe project team. The data operator and privacy contact for the project is MediGlobe (mediglobe.official@gmail.com). The operator's legal entity will be named in this policy once it is registered. The app is published on Google Play and available as a standalone Android APK.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your location or GPS coordinates (the map feature uses device location only on your device to center the map; no location data is sent to us)
  • Your medical profile (blood type, chronic conditions, allergies, emergency contact)
  • Your browsing history within the app
  • Any usage analytics or crash reports
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Google Play / RevenueCat)

5. AI Proxy and Security

AI-powered features route requests through a Cloudflare Workers proxy operated by us. This proxy:

  • Validates a request token to prevent unauthorized use
  • Rate-limits requests to 30 per minute per IP
  • Forwards requests to Anthropic over HTTPS
  • Does not log, store, or process request contents
  • Hides the Claude API key from the app binary (security measure)

6. Children's Privacy

MediGlobe is designed for travelers aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has used AI features that transmitted data, please contact us at [email protected].

7. Your Rights and Data Deletion

Since all personal data is stored locally on your device:

  • To delete all app data: Go to Profile → Reset → Reset all data. This clears all stored preferences, history, and medical profile fields.
  • To delete the app: Uninstalling MediGlobe removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • API query history: We do not store your queries on our servers. Any data retained by third-party APIs (e.g., Anthropic) is subject to their retention policies.

8. Data Protection and Your Rights (GDPR / EEA, UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, the GDPR applies to the processing of your data. Because MediGlobe works mostly offline and stores personal data on your device, the scope of processing is minimal.

  • Data controller: the MediGlobe project, contact — mediglobe.official@gmail.com (legal entity to be named once registered).
  • Legal bases: network requests to reference APIs and AI features are made to provide the feature you requested. Medical and personal data are processed only locally and are not sent to us.
  • International transfers: when you use online features, your request text may be processed by services outside the EEA (e.g. Anthropic, USA) under their own data-protection safeguards.
  • Retention: we do not store your requests on our servers and the proxy does not log them; local data is kept until you delete it.
  • Your rights: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability and objection. As data is stored locally, you exercise these directly in the app (Profile → Reset) or by uninstalling; for other requests, email us above.
  • Right to complain: you may lodge a complaint with your country's data-protection supervisory authority.
  • Automated decisions: we do not make decisions with legal effects automatically. AI answers are for reference and are not a medical decision.
