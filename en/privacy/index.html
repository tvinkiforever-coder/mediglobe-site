Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026  ·  Effective: May 29, 2026  ·  Русский

MediGlobe is an offline-first reference catalog for travelers. We collect no personal accounts, sell no data, and store sensitive health data exclusively on your device.

1. Who We Are

MediGlobe is an independent mobile application developed by David Pikalov (mediglobe.official@gmail.com). The app is published on Google Play and available as a standalone Android APK.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your location or GPS coordinates (the map feature uses device location only on your device to center the map; no location data is sent to us)
  • Your medical profile (blood type, chronic conditions, allergies, emergency contact)
  • Your browsing history within the app
  • Any usage analytics or crash reports
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Google Play / RevenueCat)

⚠️ AI Features (symptom chat, drug interactions, abroad analog search, package scanner): When you use these features, the text you type or the photo you take is sent to Anthropic Claude API via an encrypted proxy. We do not store these queries on our servers. Anthropic may process them per their privacy policy. AI features require an active internet connection and are optional — the app works fully offline without them.

5. AI Proxy and Security

AI-powered features route requests through a Cloudflare Workers proxy operated by us. This proxy:

  • Validates a request token to prevent unauthorized use
  • Rate-limits requests to 30 per minute per IP
  • Forwards requests to Anthropic over HTTPS
  • Does not log, store, or process request contents
  • Hides the Claude API key from the app binary (security measure)

6. Children's Privacy

MediGlobe is designed for travelers aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has used AI features that transmitted data, please contact us at mediglobe.official@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights and Data Deletion

Since all personal data is stored locally on your device:

  • To delete all app data: Go to Profile → Reset → Reset all data. This clears all stored preferences, history, and medical profile fields.
  • To delete the app: Uninstalling MediGlobe removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • API query history: We do not store your queries on our servers. Any data retained by third-party APIs (e.g., Anthropic) is subject to their retention policies.
